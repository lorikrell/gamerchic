Campaign Maps In-Game!

The HandyNotes - Warcraft Conquest addon provides in-game locations for campaigns, events, camps, and specific areas for those events. As changes occur through campaigns, updates can be provided to everyone with a new addon download. These can include moved locations, land destruction, fortications, and vehicle battle outcomes. Icons on the map include NPCs, buildings, enemy forces, and camps with specific notes per campaign, event, and phasing.

To add or modify locations, contact Sonceri (Discord #Loriolio#5076) with details including location, dungeon master, faction, and notes.

UPDATED for 9.0.5!
The addon is undergoing updates and additions for the next campaign in 2021!


Events In Game

Find campaign events for all factions, villains, and locations all on the map in game! You need HandyNotes and this addon to see events.

Vehicle Battles

Maps will include vehicle combat updates, including ships, airships, tanks, units, and more.

Azeroth+

Campaign maps can include Azeroth, Outlands, Draenor, and more. If it has a map ID, it can be added! Additional maps may be considered for raids and dungeons.

Downloading

The addon is available through WowUp.io addon manager and Curseforge. We are working to add new options and campaigns!

Dungeon Masters

DMs can have their events posted on the in-game map, with updates easily provided by players updating their addons.

Storytelling

As events succeed or fail, notes and icons can be added showing victories, movement of forces, and more.

Developer Guide

To keep track of changes and how to add content, the addon includes a Dev Guide in Markdown format. It includes a list of helpful addons and images.

WC Discord

Join the Warcraft Conquest Discord to learn more about events and campaigns. New DMs are always welcome!
