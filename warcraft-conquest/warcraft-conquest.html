Campaigns and Roleplay

The Warcraft Conquest Discord Server is a community project that focuses on coordinating fun and dynamic role-play plot lines set in the Warcraft universe, conducted via dungeon mastered PvE-D20, PvP-D20, RP-PvE, and RP-PvP events. We hope to provide a platform for networking and collaboration for players on the Moon Guard and Wyrmrest Accord World of Warcraft servers! Many immersive graphical and written resources have been created by members of this project that any player may use in their role-play.

Open To All

IC campaign across all US servers and factions: Alliance, Horde, and Neutral. Based on Moon Guard - US.

Dungeon Masters

We welcome new and veteran DMs to build and run events throughout the campaign, including support.

Recommended Addons

We recommend TotalRP3, Cross RP, and HandyNotes - Warcraft Conquest. Additional awesome addons include Dicemaster and Listener.

Discord & Twitter

Schedules, plans, discussions, narration updates, RP prompts, and more are tracked in the Warcraft Conquest Discord and Twitter.

Azeroth

Events take place across specific lands during each campaign. Use the HandyNotes - Warcraft Conquest addon and Discord for details.

Storytelling

Explore your character story through battles, roleplay, and events. Stock up on Elixir of Tongues!

D20 Events

We use the simplified Conquest D20 system for PVE encounters. Additional mechanics for corruption and magical items per DM.

Event Marketing

We create themed graphics for campaigns, events, vehicle battles, and loot to promote your story.

Schedule

We post daily schedules and signups to help DMs know what numbers to expect.

Maps

Maps with starting locations will be provided each day for events.

Event Types

We offer Battle, Vehicle, Scouting, Steath, Investigation, Medical, Camp, and Horror.

Huge Vehicle Battles

Tanks, Aircraft, Bombers, Massive Bosses
Conquest Warmachine system

Welcoming new Dungeons Masters!

Always wanted to write and run events? Join Warcraft Conquest! We will help you plan, design, and run your event for d20 rolling or freeform. Battles, investigations, mysteries, scouting, diplomacy, and much more!
