In Death's Shadow
Join the Fight

September 20th - 30th 2020

The eternal veil screams, torn asunder above the frozen wastes of Icecrown. Even as the shattered sky presents terrifying new threats, an old enemy has been released from the shackles that once bound them. Guided by a sinister hand from beyond, undead swarm across Azeroth to take advantage of a world weakened by strife.

The Alliance and Horde must now muster their war-wearied armies and sail north with all haste to aid the Argent Crusade in confronting the renewed Scourge onslaught. They must find some way to achieve victory, or forever be condemned to torment...In Death's Shadow

Warcraft Conquest presents In Death's Shadow, a narrative and D20 campaign that will take place between September 20th - September 30th in Northrend. This will be our final campaign in Battle for Azeroth, telling the story that leads into the Shadowlands expansion. Players from Moon Guard and Wyrmrest Accord are welcome!

Open To All

IC campaign across all US servers and factions: Alliance, Horde, and Neutral. Based on Moon Guard - US.

Dungeon Masters

We welcome new and veteran DMs to build and run events throughout the campaign.

Recommended Addons

We recommend TotalRP3, Cross RP, and HandyNotes - Warcraft Conquest. Additional awesome addons include Dicemaster and Listener.

Discord

Schedules, plans, discussions, narration updates, RP prompts, and more are tracked in the Warcraft Conquest Discord.

Northrend

Events take place across Northrend with the ultimate fight ending at the steps of Icecrown Citadel. All events will be tested for phasing.

Variety of Events

Explore your story through multiple event types including battle, stealth, scouting, medic, security, magic, vehicle, horror, and more!

D20 Events

We use the simplified Conquest D20 system for PVE encounters. Additional mechanics and campaign loot available per DM.

Vehicle Battles

We use the Conquest Warmachine system for vehicle battles in Discord, including naval, land, and air.

Schedule

Schedule of events per day. See the Preview. Will include a free day!

Maps

Maps available in Discord & addon.   Camps: Wintergarde, Agmar's Hammer, Argents.

Writing Prompts

We provide writing prompts to help track your character's story through the campaign.

Story So Far

See our Twitter and Discord for player and antagonist tales of the story so far.

Important Phasing Information!

The campaign takes place in Dragonblight and Icecrown of Northrend. Phasing is not an issue for events occuring in Dragonblight, but may require questing in Icecrown. More information will be provided. We recommend completing all Icecrown quests.
