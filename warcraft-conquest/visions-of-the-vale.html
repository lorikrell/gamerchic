Visions of the Vale
Join the Fight

May 6th - May 17th 2020

The war against the forces of N'zoth continues. As darkness spreads throughout Azeroth, the Golden Lotus of Pandaria have put out a desperate plea for aid to combat the evil that once again threatens the Vale of Eternal Blossoms. Alliance, Horde, and neutral factions suffering from losses sustained in their failed campaign in Uldum shall scrape together what forces they can to aid the Pandaren in their most desperate hour. What remains to be seen, however, is whether this effort will banish the shadow from Pandaria or crumble before the insidious schemes of N'zoth.

Warcraft Conquest presents Visions of the Vale, the next chapter in the Battle for Azeroth! Large-scale D20 events, vehicle battles, and a narrative of corruption await those who embark with the coalition forces to aid the Golden Lotus.

Open To All

IC campaign across all US servers and factions: Alliance, Horde, and Neutral. Based on Moon Guard - US.

Dungeon Masters

We welcome new and veteran DMs to build and run events throughout the campaign.

Recommended Addons

We strongly recommend TotalRP3 and Cross RP. Additional awesome addons include Dicemaster and Listener. Also stock up on Elixir of Tongues!

Discord

Schedules, plans, discussions, narration updates, RP prompts, and more are tracked in the Warcraft Conquest Discord.

Pandaria

Events take place across Pandaria with the ultimate fight ending in the Vale of Eternal Blossoms.

Horror and Corruption

Explore your story through the horrors of N'zoth. DMs provide nightmares, writing prompts, whispers, and corruption mechanics.

D20 Events

We use the simplified Conquest D20 system for PVE encounters. Additional mechanics for corruption and magical items per DM.

Vehicle Battles

We use the Conquest Warmachine system for vehicle battles in Discord, including naval, land, and air.

Schedule

Schedule of events per day. Will include a free day!

Maps

Maps with starting locations will be provided each day for events.

Event Types

We will have Battle, Vehicle, Scouting, Steath, Comedy, Puzzles, Medical, Camp, and Horror options.

Huge Vehicle Battles

Tanks, Aircraft, Bombers, Massive Bosses! Get ready for big battles!
